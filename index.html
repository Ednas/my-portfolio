The lady behind the magic!

Edna Jonsson hails from the country of fire and ice, Iceland. She moved to the sunny state of Florida when she was in high school and after graduating form the UCF Coding Bootcamp moved to Chattanooga, Tennessee.


Edna has been in real estate most of her career life, it has been a fantastic adventure helping people buy and sell homes. She is a mother of identical twin girls. In 2016 she began a career change and enrolled in the inaugural class of UCF's Coding Boot Camp, she graduated from that boot camp on September 28th, 2016. She has enjoyed the transition to coding and has enjoyed the various opportunities in this field, such as speaking at Meetups and helping other boot camp students by tutoring and advising.

Summary

I'm a fun and quirky developer, I enjoy challenges and finding solutions to problems. One of my biggest passion is to teach others. I do that through being a teaching assistant/tutor at Trilogy Education and also as facilitator of Chattanooga's Girls Who Code Club. I'm constantly learning new technologies and frameworks. The most exciting thing about coding for me is to try new things and challenge myself and then when I finally succeed at making something work, it just lights me up. I have chosen a variety of technologies to learn and have been mostly focused on JavaScript, Node.js and writing API's. I will work hard, I will continue to learn, I will continue to practice my skill and work on becoming better. That's all I can do, is improve. I really do enjoy coding, I love to solve the challenges that come with creating code and finding a good way and then a better way to do something. I focus on two things when I'm coding, first making it work and then making it look good. If I can accomplish those two things, I feel like I am making a great product.
